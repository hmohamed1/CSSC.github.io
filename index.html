Gear Tips

Prepare for a great trip with the right gear! The club provides free skis/snowboards, boots, and helmets. You’re welcome to bring your own, but make sure to pack jackets, gloves, snow pants, and goggles.

  • 🧤 Gloves

  • 🥽 Goggles

Skiing vs Snowboarding: Choose Your Adventure

Skiing

  • Easier to learn initially
  • Challenging to master advanced techniques
  • Better for exploring varied terrain quickly
  • Faster progress on day one

Beginner's Guide to Skiing

Start with the "pizza" stance for stopping. Practice on gentle slopes and gradually progress to parallel skiing. Don't forget to bend your knees and lean slightly forward!

Learning Curve

Snowboarding

  • Steeper learning curve at the start
  • Quicker to advance once basics are mastered
  • Better for freestyle and tricks
  • More comfortable boots for walking

Beginner's Guide to Snowboarding

Focus on balance and edge control. Start by learning to stand and slide on your heel edge. Be patient, as the first few days can be challenging, but progress comes quickly after!
